Feature Construction¶

Make new columns: BMI and Pulse Pressure, since both are significant indicators of heart health. -> BMI = weight(kg) / height (cm) / height (cm) x 10,000 -> Pulse Pressure = Systolic - Diastolic

Note that usually a pulse pressure of 60 or above is an indicator of poor heart health (possibiity of CVDs).
